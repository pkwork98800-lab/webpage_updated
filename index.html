If your business requires complex system integrations, custom AI workflows, or data-driven digital infrastructure rather than just a standard website, we are built for you.
While we build high-end enterprise web portals and platforms, our core expertise lies in technology consulting. We build custom AI workflow automation engines, integrate legacy ERP systems with modern dashboards, architect B2B lead generation infrastructure, and build proprietary SaaS platforms.
Because we build custom, high-impact technical systems, our projects typically span 6 to 12 weeks depending on complexity. Our engagements are value-priced based on the scope of architectural and system design required, generally ranging between ₹5 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs per system.
